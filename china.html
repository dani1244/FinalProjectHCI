MS and PHD Computer Science Scholarship in China

School of Information Science & Engineering, University of Jinan (UJN), China, is seeking highly motivated MS students for three years of studies on a Scholarship (Chinese Government Scholarship, Shandong Government Scholarship, UJN President Scholarship and other).

All studies are in ENGLISH, including ALL courses, supervision, papers, thesis and other such activities involved. Scholarship Coverage: 1) A waiver of fee for registration, tuition, internship and basic textbooks; 2) Free on-campus accommodation; 3) Medical insurance and protection scheme for international students in China; 4) Monthly living allowance (depending on qualifications and experience).

Eligibility.
